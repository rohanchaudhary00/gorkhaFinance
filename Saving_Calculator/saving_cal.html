How to use Calculator ?

  1. First select plan and according to your selected plan predicted return rate, tax, RBXS fee are choosen actomatically.

  2. Enter amount of money that you want to calculate (option1: maximum 20,000), (option2: maximum 30,000), (option3: Unlimited)

  3. Choose year.

  4. Click on calculate and it will shows: RBXS Fee, Tax, Profit, Total Return.

  5. Click on clear to remove all result.
